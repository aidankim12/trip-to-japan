UNC Summer in Tokyo 2023

Despite the name of the study abroad program, the location of the program was actually in Chiba. During the 7-weeks, students were able to take two levels of language classes while having plenty of opportunities to learn about Japanese culture via homestays, excursions to different cities, activities with university students, and more.
